The Dovahkiin costume from The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim recreates the iconic look of the Dragonborn. The horned iron helmet, fur cloak, and scaled armor are all crafted with careful attention to the in-game original.

The helmet is sculpted from EVA foam with thermoplastic horns. The armor is assembled from individual scale plates that mimic the steel armor from the game. The cloak features real fur for an authentic Nordic look.

The costume is perfectly suited for outdoor shoots, where the atmosphere of Skyrim's cold north feels most natural.
