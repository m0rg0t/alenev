The Advanced Suit costume of Isaac Clarke from Dead Space 2 is a project that combined several technologies: EVA foam, 3D printing, and LED lighting. The iconic engineering suit from the space horror game required a special approach to convey its futuristic aesthetic.

The helmet was manufactured using 3D printing for precise shaping, with built-in blue LED visor lighting. Chest and shoulder armor pieces are cut from EVA foam and coated with multiple paint layers to create a worn metal effect.

The detailed build process is documented in a guide on [Instructables](https://www.instructables.com/Dead-Space-2-Cosplay-Creating-the-Advanced-Suit-Su/).
