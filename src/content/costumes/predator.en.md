The Predator costume is one of the most technically challenging projects. Creating the look of the iconic alien hunter from the Predator franchise required working with latex, silicone, and a complex lighting system.

The mask is made from latex with detailed skin texture and dreadlocks. The chest armor and pauldrons are crafted from EVA foam with metallic paint finish. The shoulder-mounted plasma caster features LED lighting.

The costume was showcased at several events and photo shoots, consistently drawing attention with its level of detail.
