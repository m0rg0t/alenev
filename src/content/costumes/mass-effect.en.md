The Mass Effect costume recreates the N7 combat armor, familiar to every fan of BioWare's trilogy. The red-and-white color scheme with the signature N7 stripe is instantly recognizable.

All armor elements are cut from EVA foam and carefully fitted. The paint job uses multiple layers applied with an airbrush for smooth gradients. Details are enhanced with LED lighting in the characteristic Mass Effect blue and orange tones.

The costume was used in a travel photo shoot, allowing the character to be shown in unusual yet atmospheric locations.
