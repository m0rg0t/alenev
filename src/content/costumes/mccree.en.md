The Jesse McCree costume from Overwatch became one of the most ambitious projects. The cowboy look from Blizzard's universe required meticulous attention to every detail -- from the iconic Peacekeeper revolver to the chest armor and cape.

The armor base is made from EVA foam with thermoforming and painting. Special attention was given to the signature BAMF belt buckle and bracers. The cape is sewn from heavy fabric with a weathered effect for an authentic look.

The costume was featured in several photo shoots at various locations, including a unique underwater session.
