**ASIAVOL [V] 2019** was the fifth annual cosplay festival, held on **January 27, 2019 in Vologda, Russia**. Anton Lenev participated as a **jury member** of the competition program. For the jury's stage defile he appeared in his own **McCree from Overwatch** costume.

## From the official jury announcement (festival organizers)

> 🔥 It's about to get hot! Another jury member! ⚡ The wonderful and one-of-a-kind — **Anton Lenev** ⚡
>
> A master of truly strong masculine looks, a talented crafter and an incredibly friendly guy — coming to us from the lovely city of Rybinsk!
>
> 🌟 **Winner of such festivals as:** AvaExpo 2015 (special prize from the Ucon festival), FAP 2015 (1st place — Space Opera defile), FAP 2016 (1st place — Western defile, audience choice), Starcon 2016 (special prize), FanFan 2018 (special prize). 🌟
>
> Also a **regular participant** of: Igromir, ComicCon Russia, Starcon, AvaExpo, and FAP!
>
> Anton improves every year, creating ever more detailed and interesting characters. This guy has golden hands — he builds the crafts for all his own projects.

— [Announcement post on VK, January 22, 2019](https://vk.com/wall-172806430_919)
