**FAP 2019 (Festival of Asian Popular Culture)** was a cosplay and pop-culture festival held on May 11, 2019 at the A. M. Dobrynin Palace of Culture in Yaroslavl, Russia. Anton Lenev took home awards in two different nominations at this festival.

## 🏆 "Photocosplay" — 1st place

Anton took **1st place** in the **"Photocosplay"** nomination with a series of shots as **McCree from Overwatch**. The photographer was **Slava Grebenkin** (Rybinsk).

Full nomination results (per the [VK announcement](https://vk.com/wall-174227268_445)):

- 🥇 **1st place** — Anton, photo by Slava Grebenkin — Overwatch (Rybinsk)
- 🥈 **2nd place** — AliceYuric, photo by Denis Krasilnikov — *Batman Returns* (Yaroslavl)
- 🥉 **3rd place** — sweet__loli, photo by Nikita Kraev — DC, Harley Quinn (Moscow)
- 👥 **Audience choice** — Alice Rammsteiner & Mist, photo by KeyDou — *Avatar: The Last Airbender* (Nizhny Novgorod)

## 👥 "Alternative Solo Defile" — Audience choice

In the **"Alternative Solo Defile"** nomination Anton won the **Audience choice** award for his on-stage performance as **Thor from *Thor: Ragnarok*** (Marvel).

Full nomination results (per the [VK announcement](https://vk.com/wall-174227268_551)):

- 🥇 **1st place** — Yuna — *Snow White / Mirror Mirror* (Moscow)
- 🥈 **2nd place** — sweet__loli — *Wonder Woman / DC* (Moscow)
- 🥉 **3rd place** — Magert — *Princess Tiabeanie / Disenchantment* (Moscow)
- 👥 **Audience choice** — Anton — *Thor / Marvel — Thor: Ragnarok* (Rybinsk)
