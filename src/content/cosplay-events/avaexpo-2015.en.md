**AvaExpo 2015** was an anime and pop-culture festival in Saint Petersburg. Anton Lenev took home a **🏆 special prize from the Ucon festival** as **Dovahkiin** (*The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim*).

This win is mentioned in the [official ASIAVOL [V] 2019 jury announcement](https://vk.com/wall-172806430_919) introducing Anton as a member of the jury.
