The cosplay competition final of the **Ohtacon** festival was held on March 7, 2020 at the Okhta Mall in Saint Petersburg, as part of the MicroComicon geek fair. 28 cosplayers from across Russia competed, with 12 advancing to the final.

Anton Lenev performed as **Thor from Thor: Ragnarok** and took home two awards:

- 🏆 **"Best Cosplay"** — the festival's main jury category ([Tribuna.ee report](https://tribuna.ee/attributes/kosplay/) by Arseny Denisov).
- 🏆 **"Best Comics Costume"** — a special prize from [Comilfo Publishing House](https://vk.com/wall-36491054_13471) with a full bundle of comic books as the reward.

Photos in the gallery below come from Anton's VK page ([post from April 24, 2020](https://vk.com/wall4443423_2491)), shot by **Slava Romanov**. The award-ceremony shots are from Comilfo Publishing's page.
