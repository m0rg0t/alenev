IgroMir 2018 was Russia's largest gaming expo, held at Crocus Expo. The McCree costume from Overwatch was showcased at the event. The exhibition brought together thousands of gaming and cosplay enthusiasts, making it an excellent venue for showing off the costume and connecting with like-minded people.
