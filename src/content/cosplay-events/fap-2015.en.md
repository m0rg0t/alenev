**FAP 2015 (Festival of Asian Popular Culture)** was a cosplay and pop-culture festival in Yaroslavl. Anton Lenev took **🏆 1st place in the "Space Opera" defile** as **Commander Shepard** from *Mass Effect*.

This win is mentioned in the [official ASIAVOL [V] 2019 jury announcement](https://vk.com/wall-172806430_919) introducing Anton as a member of the jury.
